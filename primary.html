ABOUT ROVINGHEIGHTS

Rovingheights bookstore was co-founded
by the trio of Adedotun Eyinade, Oluwatobi
Eyinade and Adebola Eyinade. Yes, we are
a family-owned business. From the dining room
of our Lagos mainland flat in 2015, we have
built Rovingheights bookstore into one of the
most recognizable brands in the Nigerian book
industry and we are just getting started.
